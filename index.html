A maximum flow rate of 2.5 gallons per minute (GPM) has been the standard for new showerheads in the US since the Energy Policy Act of 1992. This can be observed at Max Flow Standard. Some states have even stricter limits on GPM. For example, states like California, Oregon, and Hawaii have set the maximum at: 1.8 GPM. This can be found at: Requirements by State.
